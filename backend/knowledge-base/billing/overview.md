## Understanding Minutes

### What Counts as a Minute?

- Each minute of call time (rounded up)
- Both inbound and outbound calls
- Includes AI processing time

### What Doesn't Count?

- Failed calls (no answer, busy)
- Setup and configuration time
- Listening to recordings

### Overage Pricing

When you exceed included minutes:
- Additional minutes: $0.05/minute
- Or purchase credits in advance

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment details
5. Upgrade takes effect immediately

### What Happens When You Upgrade?

- New limits apply immediately
- Unused minutes don't roll over
- New features unlock instantly
- Prorated billing for the current period

## Downgrading

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select lower tier
4. Downgrade takes effect next billing cycle

### What Happens When You Downgrade?

- Current plan continues until period ends
- Excess agents become inactive (not deleted)
- Campaigns over limit are paused
- Premium features become unavailable

## Billing FAQs

### When am I billed?

Monthly, on the anniversary of your signup date.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime. Access continues until period ends.

### Do minutes roll over?

No, included minutes reset each billing cycle. Credits do carry over.

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Invoice (Enterprise only)

### How do I get a receipt?

Receipts are emailed automatically. Also available in Settings → Billing.

### Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is available indefinitely. No credit card required.
