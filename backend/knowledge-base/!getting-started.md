# Welcome to Gleam

Welcome to **Gleam** - your AI-powered voice agent platform. This knowledge base will help you get started and make the most of your AI calling capabilities.

## What is Gleam?

Gleam is a comprehensive SaaS platform that enables you to create and deploy AI voice agents capable of handling both inbound and outbound phone calls with natural conversation. Our platform combines:

- **Real-time Voice Processing** - Sub-500ms latency for natural conversations
- **Advanced AI Models** - Powered by GPT-4o, Deepgram, and ElevenLabs
- **Flexible Deployment** - Handle inbound calls, run outbound campaigns, or both

### Step 2: Create Your First Agent

1. Navigate to **Agents** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Agent**
3. Choose a template or start from scratch
4. Configure your agent's voice, personality, and behavior
5. Save and activate your agent

[Learn more about Creating Agents â†’](/dashboard/knowledge-base?path=agents/creating-agents)

### Step 3: Get a Phone Number

1. Go to **Phone Numbers** in the sidebar
2. Click **Buy Number** to purchase a Twilio number
3. Assign the number to your agent

### Step 4: Start Making Calls

- **For Inbound**: Share your number and start receiving calls
- **For Outbound**: Use the dialpad or create a campaign
